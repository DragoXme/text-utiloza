Overview

Text Utiloza is designed around practical online text tools. Text tools process text in your browser whenever possible.

Information we collect

The current site does not include accounts, payment forms, or a custom backend database. If you contact us by email, your message and email address may be used to reply.

Service providers

Like most websites, this site may rely on service providers that process standard technical information such as IP address, browser details, and request logs to deliver, secure, and improve the site.

Cookies, analytics, and ads

Text Utiloza may use Google AdSense to show ads. Google and its partners may use cookies, web beacons, IP address, browser details, and similar identifiers to serve ads, measure ad performance, prevent abuse, and keep the service reliable. You can learn how Google uses data from sites and apps at Google's partner sites policy.

The text tools remain usable without creating an account. Text entered into browser-side tools stays on your device unless a page clearly says otherwise.

Tool inputs

Do not enter sensitive personal, financial, medical, legal, or confidential information into any online tool unless you understand how the page works and trust the device you are using.
