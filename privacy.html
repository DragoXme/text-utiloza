Overview

Text Utiloza is designed around practical online text tools. When tools are added, they should process text in your browser whenever possible.

Information we collect

The current site does not include accounts, payment forms, or a custom backend database. If you contact us by email, your message and email address may be used to reply.

Tool inputs

Do not paste sensitive personal, financial, medical, legal, or confidential information into any online tool unless you fully understand how that tool works.

Like most websites, this site may rely on service providers that process standard technical information such as IP address, browser details, and request logs to deliver and secure the site.

Cookies, analytics, and ads

The current version does not require cookies for core browsing. If analytics or advertising services are added, this policy should be updated with details about cookies, personalization, and opt-out options.
